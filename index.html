How I Work

01
Discovery & Brief

I start by understanding your goals, audience, and vision. Whether it's a video edit, design piece, or UI screen — knowing the why behind the project shapes every creative decision I make.

02
Concept & Planning

Before touching any tool, I sketch the structure — storyboard for videos, wireframes for UI, mood boards for design. A clear plan means fewer revisions and faster delivery.

03
Creation & Execution

This is where the magic happens. I execute with precision — editing, designing, animating — with constant attention to detail, pacing, color, and visual consistency.

04
Review & Refinement

I welcome feedback and iterate until the output feels right. Good work doesn't happen in the first draft — it's refined through honest review and careful adjustments.

05
Final Delivery

Clean, export-ready files delivered in your preferred format. I make sure everything is organized, labeled, and easy to use — because the handoff is part of the work.
